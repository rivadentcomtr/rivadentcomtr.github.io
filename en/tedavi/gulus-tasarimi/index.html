Smile Design

Smile Design makes your dental condition look more aesthetically pleasing and more natural. Aesthetic Smile Design: Besides being a physiological action that most clearly reveals some clues about your emotional world and our emotional state, it is one of the most important communication tools compatible with the characteristic structure of your face.

With Smile Aesthetics, all surgical and aesthetic interventions for teeth and gums are aimed at obtaining the most beautiful smile. With “White Aesthetics”, it is aimed to give an aesthetic appearance to the gums with “Pink Aesthetics”, with the aesthetic appearance of the teeth with the procedures to be performed on the teeth.

Contents

Smile Design and Facial Aesthetics

First of all, the characteristic structure of your face is evaluated; a personalized aesthetic smile is designed with the aim of being in harmony with the eyes, skin color and lip structure. In our clinic, our treatment methods and technical infrastructure regarding smile aesthetics are very strong. With Smile Design, a special smile is designed for each of our patients and the treatment process is started to achieve an aesthetic smile result.

Even though this is a series of treatments and operations, it is actually an artistic process; an aesthetic smile that we bring in the process of smile design is also an aesthetic touch to human life. We aim for our patients who come to our clinic for smile aesthetics to care about their own smiles, as well as for them to have an aesthetic smile.

Aesthetic Smile Design

Smile design does not only give individuals a healthy and aesthetic smile; it reflects the person’s mental state, happiness, joy and satisfaction to the outside world in the most aesthetic and impressive way, with a clear expression.

If the patient has a dental and oral problem in an aesthetic sense, this causes him to put barriers between himself and his environment, to feel inadequate in terms of self-confidence, and even these individuals may restrict their smiles and suppress all their sincerity as a result of these feelings.

In this context, pink aesthetics with smile design is not only a local aesthetic operation, but also an aesthetic dentistry treatment procedure that reflects the emotional state of individuals and makes your dental condition look more aesthetically pleasing and more natural in an artistic sense.

With the help of zirconium tooth coating, porcelain lamina veneer or leaf porcelain tooth, bonding filling, artenemal technique and implant treatments, patients are regained the self-confidence lost with an aesthetic smile through procedures performed within the body of other aesthetic dentistry.

What are Smile Design Treatment Methods?

In Smile Design, there is a healthy tooth and gingival structure in the background of an aesthetic smile, the aesthetic appearance of the teeth and gums of the patients; at the same time, it is an indication of having a healthy mouth structure, teeth and gums.

A mouth structure that has lost its health does not display an aesthetically pleasing appearance. At this point, white aesthetic and pink aesthetic treatment methods show themselves, all smile aesthetics and smile design treatment methods for teeth are generally referred to as “White Aesthetics”.

Smile Design and Our Options

People can apply to pink aesthetic and white aesthetic treatment methods in order to feel better and have an aesthetic smile, so clinically, we apply some surgical and non-surgical treatment methods for a healthy and aesthetically successful smile of our patients.

White Aesthetics

The main white aesthetic treatment methods we apply for oral health and aesthetic integrity in our clinic;

Within the scope of smile design, methods such as zirconium tooth coating treatment, bonding, implant in one day, laminated tooth coating and leaf porcelain teeth, artenemal bonding technique are applied in our clinic. While dental calculus complaints, which are directly related to dental and gingival health and cause extremely serious problems in terms of pink aesthetics and white aesthetics, are eliminated with interventions; In the smile design treatment process, successful results are achieved in the name of aesthetic appearance by whitening the tooth surface, and all these applications restore the self-confidence they lost to our patients, remove the obstacles in front of their smiles and give them the aesthetic smile they long for.

What procedures are performed for Smile Aesthetics?

Laughing is the best action given to human beings. Nothing should hinder a sincere smile. Smile Design Applications

Clinically, we carry out various applications to ensure the smile aesthetics of our patients, with pink aesthetic treatment methods, by showing the same attention to dental health and aesthetic appearance, as well as to gingival aesthetics. Gums are the vital areas that protect and nourish the roots of the teeth, many dental aesthetics and smile aesthetic problems are due to problems in the gums. These problems necessitate pink aesthetic treatments because of the aesthetic losses caused in the gingiva.

Again, a gingiva that has lost its retention due to wear or other reasons has difficulty in retaining the teeth; for this reason, a negative image occurs in terms of dental aesthetics and smile aesthetics, as the teeth will wobble or lose. Gingival recessions and melting; since it will disrupt the linear line at the junction of the tooth and gingiva, it also negatively affects the aesthetic appearance of the teeth and smile aesthetics.

While all these negative gingival conditions cause the gingiva to lose its aesthetic appearance, the concept of “Pink Aesthetics” comes into play here. If we accept the gums as a basic structure in which the teeth live, we have chosen the simplest and most accurate expression to emphasize the importance of pink aesthetics. Therefore, it is a very healthy approach for patients to preserve this basic structure in the best way and to benefit from professional clinical interventions at certain intervals.

Why is Smile Design Important?

Gingival problems with delayed treatment cause some loss over time, and therefore the presence of teeth is also dangerous. Problems such as deterioration of tooth alignment, gingival recession or calculus formation cause the gingiva to have an aesthetically deprived appearance; after the elimination of such problems that destroy the gums and cause losses, the pink aesthetic to be applied will also lead to an improvement in terms of appearance.

frequently asked questions

Typically, you won't feel pain during dental treatment. Local anesthesia is applied to numb the area before the procedure. After the anesthesia wears off, you might experience mild soreness, but this is temporary.

Teeth whitening is completely safe when done with proper techniques and under the supervision of a qualified dentist. It does not harm your oral health. However, some temporary sensitivity may occur.

Teeth cleaning is very important for preventing gum diseases and maintaining oral health. Tartar buildup can lead to gum inflammation, so regular dental cleanings are recommended.

A dental implant is a titanium post placed in the jawbone to replace a lost tooth. It serves as a stable foundation for a dental crown, providing functionality similar to natural teeth.

The duration of orthodontic treatment varies based on individual needs, but it typically lasts between 1 to 3 years. The treatment time depends on the condition of the teeth and how well you follow the treatment plan.

Children's dental treatment is personalized according to their age and development. Regular check-ups and dental cleanings are essential for maintaining their oral health and preventing future issues.

Orthodontic treatment is usually recommended for children between the ages of 9 and 14, when their growth phase is not complete. However, adults can also undergo orthodontic treatment, and treatment can be effective even after the growth period.

Tooth decay is treated depending on its severity. Superficial cavities are treated with fillings, while deeper cavities may require root canal treatment.

Dental prostheses, such as crowns or bridges, are used to replace missing teeth. They can be recommended for patients with tooth loss to restore both function and aesthetics.

It is recommended to visit the dentist at least twice a year for regular check-ups. Regular visits help with early detection of potential issues and are essential for maintaining optimal oral health.

Gum bleeding is typically a sign of gum inflammation and should not be ignored. You should visit a dentist if you experience gum bleeding to address any potential oral health issues.

The recovery process depends on the type of treatment. For procedures like fillings and cleanings, recovery is usually immediate. However, for treatments like implants or tooth extractions, recovery may take a few days.

Urgent dental treatments can be performed during pregnancy. However, it is generally best to schedule dental procedures during the second trimester (3-6 months). Be sure to discuss anesthesia and medication use with your dentist.

Tooth sensitivity is often caused by enamel erosion or gum recession. Your dentist may recommend treatments to reduce sensitivity, and using sensitivity-reducing toothpaste can also help alleviate discomfort.
