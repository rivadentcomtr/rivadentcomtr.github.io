Resume

Dt. Muhammet Alptekin Özbek

Dt. Muhammet Alptekin Özbek was born in Bayburt in 1996 and completed his primary, secondary, and high school education in Bayburt. After graduating from the Faculty of Dentistry at Medipol University in 2020, he began working at Beşiroğlu Oral and Dental Health Center. He performed various dental treatments there for two years before joining the Rivadent Maltepe Clinic in July 2022.

Education and Areas of Expertise

Dt. Muhammet Alptekin Özbek specializes in aesthetic dentistry, prosthetic dental treatments, oral and maxillofacial surgery, and implantology. With the knowledge and experience gained in these fields, he provides the latest treatment methods to his patients.

After graduating, Dt. Muhammet Alptekin Özbek worked for two years at Beşiroğlu Oral and Dental Health Center, where he carried out various clinical treatments. In 2022, he continued his professional career at Rivadent Maltepe Clinic, where he offers comprehensive oral health services and advanced treatments to his patients. 

Dt. Muhammet Alptekin Özbek utilizes the latest technologies and materials to provide aesthetic restorations, functional prosthetics, and surgical dental treatments. By focusing on implantology and restorative dental care, he develops personalized solutions tailored to each patient’s needs.

Certifications and Achievements:

Dt. Muhammet Alptekin Özbek holds over 10 national and international certificates and achievement awards in fields such as aesthetic dentistry, implantology, and restorative dental treatments. These certifications demonstrate his commitment to continuous professional development and his passion for staying current with the latest innovations in the dental field.
