Resume

Dr. Dt. Esra Duran

Dr. Dt. Esra Duran completed her 5-year undergraduate education at the Ondokuz Mayıs University Faculty of Dentistry in 2006. In 2014, she successfully completed her doctoral education at the Department of Endodontics, Ondokuz Mayıs University Faculty of Dentistry. This educational journey has provided her with in-depth knowledge of dental health and treatment methods.

Dr. Dt. Esra Duran began her professional career in 2011 at the Ordu - Korgan District State Hospital, where she served for approximately one year. Until March 2016, she worked at the Samsun Oral and Dental Health Hospital, gaining experience in a variety of dental treatment procedures.

Dr. Dt. Esra Duran has extensive expertise in endodontics (root canal therapy) and provides customized treatment plans for her patients using modern treatment methods. With her clinical experience and professional background, she prioritizes patient satisfaction in dental health.
